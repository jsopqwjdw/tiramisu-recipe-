Tiramisu Recipe

About Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that translates to "pick me up." It is a delightful combination of coffee-soaked ladyfingers layered with a rich mascarpone cream and topped with cocoa powder. This dessert is perfect for coffee and dessert lovers alike!

Ingredients

6 large egg yolks
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2/3 cup milk
1 1/4 cups heavy cream
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
8 ounces mascarpone cheese
1 cup brewed espresso or strong coffee, cooled
1/4 cup coffee liqueur (optional)
24-30 ladyfingers
2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
